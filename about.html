Kart School

At the Carlisle Racing School you’ll gain experience and learn the techniques to become a better and faster driver. Courses are customized to the individual karter; from absolute beginners to long-time karters. We can tailor a course to meet your needs.

Beginning karters will enjoy the driving lessons while experienced karters will learn from our advanced chassis setup class, perfect for the karter returning to the sport after several years away.

A one-to-one instructor-to-student ratio guarantees personal attention and the best possible learning experience available.

For more information, email info@KartTrack.com or call 517-282-9387.
